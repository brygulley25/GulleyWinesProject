About Us

Gulley Wine is a family-owned winery located in the heart of the California wine country. We pride ourselves on producing high-quality wines that are crafted with care and attention to detail. Our commitment to quality extends from the vineyard to the bottle, and we believe that every glass of Gulley Wine tells a story.

Our Wine Making Process

At Gulley Wine, we believe that the best wines are made in the vineyard. Our winemakers work closely with our grape growers to ensure that every vine is tended to with care and attention to detail. Once the grapes are harvested, they are gently pressed and the juice is aged in oak barrels to create the rich, complex flavors that our wines are known for.

Our Winery

Our winery is located in the heart of the California wine country, surrounded by rolling hills and stunning vineyards. We offer tours and tastings to our visitors, and we are proud to be a part of this vibrant and growing wine community.
